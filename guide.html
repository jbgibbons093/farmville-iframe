🏡 Welcome to Hughberry Farms!

Welcome, farmer! Hughberry Farms is a relaxing farming simulation where you'll grow crops, raise animals, craft goods, and build your farming empire. This guide will teach you everything you need to know to become a master farmer!

💡 Quick Tip: Press I to open your inventory and manage your farm. Press L to toggle this guide on/off!
🌱 Getting Started

When you first arrive at Hughberry Farms, you'll start at Level 1 with some starting gold. Here's what you should do first:

  1. Buy Seeds - Visit the Seed Store in the marketplace to purchase Wheat and Carrot seeds
  2. Plant Your Crops - Go to the Crops tab or walk to a plot in the 3D world and plant your seeds
  3. Buy Equipment - Visit the Blueprint Store to buy a Milk Pail (required to work with cows)
  4. Start Your Animals - Go to the Animals tab and click "Start" on a cow to begin milk production
  5. Wait & Collect - Crops and animals take time. When ready, harvest crops and collect products!
⚠️ Important: You need the correct equipment to work with each animal type. Buy equipment from the Blueprint Store before trying to start animal production!
🌾 Growing Crops

Crops are your bread and butter (literally - you can craft both!). Here's how the crop system works:

How to Plant Crops
  1. Buy seeds from the Seed Store in the marketplace
  2. Go to the Crops tab in the inventory menu
  3. Find an empty plot and select which crop to plant
  4. Click "Plant" to plant the seed
  5. Wait for the timer to complete (shown on each plot)
  6. When ready, go to the plot in the 3D world and press X to harvest

🐄 Raising Animals

Animals produce valuable goods like milk, eggs, wool, and more. Each animal type requires specific equipment before you can work with them.

How Animal Production Works
  1. Buy the required equipment from the Blueprint Store
  2. Go to the Animals tab and click "Start" on an idle animal
  3. Wait for the production timer to complete
  4. When ready, walk to the collection station in the 3D world and press X
  5. All ready products from that animal type will be collected at once!

⚒️ Crafting System

Turn your raw crops and animal products into valuable crafted goods! Crafted items sell for much more than raw materials and give bonus XP.

How to Craft
  1. Buy the recipe from the Blueprint Store (each recipe costs gold and has a level requirement)
  2. Gather the required ingredients (crops, animal products, or other crafted items)
  3. Visit the Crafting Table in the marketplace
  4. Select your recipe and click "Craft"
  5. Sell your crafted goods at the Sell Goods stall for profit!

📝 Tips & Strategies
Early Game (Levels 1-2)
  • Focus on Wheat - It's cheap, grows fast (30s), and you'll need lots for Flour
  • Buy the Milk Pail first - It's only 50g and lets you work with all 6 cows
  • Start multiple animals at once - Get all cows and chickens producing while you farm
  • Use Auto-Replant - Set it to Wheat and let your farm run automatically
  • Buy the Flour recipe early - 3 Wheat makes 1 Flour, which sells for 20g and gives 10 XP
Mid Game (Levels 3-4)
  • Diversify your crops - You'll need Potatoes, Carrots, and Corn for Stew
  • Get all equipment - Each new animal type = more passive income
  • Start crafting chains - Make Flour first, then use it for Bread and Pies
  • Beehives are valuable - 9 hives producing honey makes great Mead!
Late Game (Levels 5+)
  • Pumpkins are king - 15g seed cost, 30g sell price = 100% profit, plus great for Pies
  • Cake is the ultimate craft - Requires Flour + Butter + 2 Eggs, sells for 100g!
  • Run all animals constantly - 36 animals producing = massive passive income
  • Keep seeds stocked - Buy in bulk (5 at a time) to keep your farm running

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Why can't I start an animal?

You need to buy the correct equipment from the Blueprint Store first. Each animal type requires specific equipment (e.g., Milk Pail for cows, Bee Suit for beehives).

Q: Why can't I plant a certain crop?

Check two things: 1) Do you have seeds? Buy them from the Seed Store. 2) Is the crop unlocked? Some crops require higher levels (check the Unlocks tab).

Q: Why can't I craft an item?

You need to buy the recipe first from the Blueprint Store. Also make sure you have all the required ingredients - some recipes need other crafted items!

Q: How do I collect animal products?

Walk to the collection station for that animal type (e.g., Honey Jar for beehives, Milk Pail station for cows) and press X. All ready products will be collected at once!

Q: My crops/animals show "Ready" but nothing happens?

You need to physically go to the location in the 3D world and press X to interact. The menu shows status but you collect in the 3D world!
